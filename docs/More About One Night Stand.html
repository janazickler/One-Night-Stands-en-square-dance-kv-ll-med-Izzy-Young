More About One Night Stands

This short publication is copyrighted by Israel G. Young and the Folklore Centrum in 1983.
It is written by Izzy Young, and illustrated by Agneta Svensk. It contains a detailed
description of 25 different folk dances, their move/steps, as well as, their accompanying
lyrics and music.

It is primarily written in Swedish, though many English descriptions and expressions are included.

This publication was printed by Izzy Young and distributed by the Folklore Centrum. It has
not been available for sale for more than a decade, and as it stands now, the Folklore Centrum
is permanently closed. This publication does not appear in public library searches, and this
is the first time it has been digitized, and made available to the public.
